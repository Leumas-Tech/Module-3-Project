Covid Express

About Us

Covid Express is a search engine used to find Covid-19 statistics by country, pull number of people infected, find out the amount of new cases and number of deaths.

Mission

Our mission is to have people informed about past and current cases worldwide. People are also able to search for hospitals, the symptom checker will look for the nearest hospital by location and locate it in google maps.
